[−][src]Module uom::si::thermal_conductivity

Thermal conductivity (base unit watt per meter kelvin, kg · m · s⁻³ · K⁻¹).

Thermal conductivity has the same kind as temperature interval, as this quantity relates to change of temperature. Not of kind TemperatureKind, used by thermodynamic temperature. See thermodynamic temperature for a full explanation.
